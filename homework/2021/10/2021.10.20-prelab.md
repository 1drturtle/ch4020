- \(M_1V_1 = M_2V_2\)

- b) \(M_1 = 0.850 M\), \((0.850 M)(x L) = (0.020 M)(0.500 L), x = 1.12\cdot10^{-2}\) L. They can add \(x = 1.12\cdot10^{-2}\) liters of the stock solution to create their solution.
